About Us

Who We Are

InAmigos Foundation was founded by Mr. Govind Shukla on September 23, 2020. Headquartered in Sipat Ujwal Nagar, Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh, we are a Central Government-licensed Section 8 non-profit committed to building an inclusive, compassionate, and empowered society.

We hold 80G & 12A certifications ensuring donor tax benefits and full accountability. As a CSR-1 and NITI Aayog registered body, we collaborate with corporate partners and align our mission with national development goals. Our IAF ISO 9001:2015 certification reflects our commitment to quality.

Section 8 Registered 80G & 12A Certified CSR-1 Registered NITI Aayog ISO 9001:2015
Every contribution is directed towards essential causes — food, education, women empowerment, animal welfare, and environmental sustainability across India.

— Mr. Govind Shukla, Founder & CEO

🍱 01
Food & Clothing

Project Seva

Serving the underprivileged with hot meals, clean water, and clothing drives — because dignity begins with basic needs. Our volunteers conduct regular distribution camps across communities in need, ensuring no one sleeps hungry.

📚02
Education

Project Bachpanshala

Nurturing young minds through quality education for underprivileged children. We run learning centres and provide resources to bridge the education gap.

🐾03
Animal Welfare

Project Jeev

Animal rescue, protection, feeding, and medical care. Because compassion knows no species — we believe all living beings deserve safety and dignity.

👩‍💼04
Women Empowerment

Project Udaan

Skill development and financial independence for women — soaring towards a brighter future through training programs, microenterprise support, and social inclusion.

🌱05
Environment

Project Prakriti

Tree plantation, environmental conservation, and sustainability drives — planting seeds for a greener tomorrow and protecting nature for future generations.

🎓06
Skill Development

Project Vikas

Enhancing employability through vocational skill development programs, career guidance, and training — bridging the gap between potential and opportunity.

Social Impact

Why Our Work Matters

🍽️

Fighting Hunger & Poverty

Thousands of families across 28 states receive regular food and essential supplies through Project Seva, reducing malnutrition and restoring dignity.

📖

Breaking the Education Barrier

Bachpanshala brings learning to children who would otherwise be left behind, creating pathways out of poverty through quality education.

Empowering Half the Sky

Project Udaan helps women achieve economic independence — transforming individual lives and entire communities through skill building.

🌍

Healing Our Planet

Prakriti's conservation drives and mass tree plantations contribute to a healthier environment, aligning grassroots action with sustainability goals.

🐕

Giving a Voice to the Voiceless
